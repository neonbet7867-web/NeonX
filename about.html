My Process

1

Understanding Your Idea

First, I listen to you. I want to understand exactly what you need, who your audience is, and what kind of design you like. This helps me build exactly what you have in mind.

2

Planning the Design

Before coding, I plan the structure. I decide where the images, text, and buttons will go to ensure the website looks good and makes sense for the user.

This is where the magic happens. I write the code using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to bring the design to life, focusing on clean and error-free coding.
